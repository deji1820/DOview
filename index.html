Offense Level Reference
Level 1
Negligence to Fulfill Basic Duties
  • Tardiness
  • Unexpected absences
  • Wearing inappropriate attire
  • Failure to submit correspondence
  • Sleeping in class
  • Violation of SOPs
  • Misbehavior
  • Improper haircut
  • No ID or No gate pass
  • Loitering during class hours
  • Littering
  • Trespassing
  • Disruption of classes
Level 2
Minor Behavioral Infractions
  • Any disruptive behavior/material
  • Using vulgar language
  • Bringing electronic gadgets
  • Staying after school hours
  • Putting temporary marks on body
  • Wearing make-up or nail polish
  • Fancy haircut or hair color
  • Unauthorized meetings/rallies
  • Vandalism
Level 3
Acts of Disrespect & Dishonesty
  • Any form of disrespect or malicious act
  • Tampering of school documents
  • Forgery / identity theft
  • Bringing cigarettes or lighter
  • Lending of IDs and gate pass
  • Cutting classes
  • Inflicting harm to self
  • Stealing or attempted theft
  • Bringing pornographic materials
  • Bringing gambling materials
  • Assault or threat to authority
Level 4
Acts Unbecoming of an HIMC Student
  • Any form of cheating
  • Any kind of bullying
  • Violation of Data Privacy Act
  • Violation of Child Protection Policy
  • Threatening / harassment
  • Smoking / vaping
  • Extortion
  • Under influence of drugs or alcohol
  • Initiating fights or gambling
  • Backbiting or rumor mongering
  • Possession of deadly weapons
