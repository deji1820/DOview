The HIMC Disciplinary Measures

LEVEL 1 - Negligence to Fulfill Basic Duties

  • Tardiness
  • Unexpected absences
  • Wearing inappropriate attire
  • Failure to submit correspondence / have materials a 3rd time
  • Sleeping in class
  • Violation of SOP's
  • Misbehavior
  • Improper haircut
  • No ID or No gate pass
  • Loitering during class hours
  • Littering
  • Trespassing
  • Disruption of classes.

Penalties for Level 1 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Reminder + 1 demerit
  • 2nd Offense - Reminder + 2 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Reprimand + 3 demerits
  • 4th Offense onwards - Parent Notification + 4 demerits

LEVEL 2 - Minor Behavioral Infractions or Violations of Basic School Rules and Regulations

  • Any disruptive behavior/audio/video/material or physical appearance which distracts/affects co-attendees.
  • Using vulgar language or indecent publication online or behavior offline
  • Bringing of electronic gadgets (i.e. cell phones, iPad, iPod, digital camera, etc.)
  • Staying in the school premises after school hours (a student has 30 minutes to go home after dismissed)
  • Putting temporary marks on their bodies
  • Putting on make-up or colored nail polish
  • Fancy haircut, fancy or loud hair color, and hair with highlights
  • Holding unauthorized meetings, rallies, or demonstrations
  • Vandalism

Penalties for Level 2 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Reminder + 2 demerit
  • 2nd Offense - Reminder + 4 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Reprimand + 6 demerits
  • 4th Offense onwards - Parent Notification + Deprivation from attending the subject where offense was committed + 8 demerits

LEVEL 3 - Acts of Disrespect and Dishonesty

  • Any form of disrespect, malicious act, immorality or unwanted gesture online & offline.
  • Tampering of any school document/property or attempt to hack the HIMC system (Student must pay for any / replace any broken items)
  • Any form of dishonesty, like (but not limited to) forgery, online identity theft, etc.
  • Bringing of cigarettes, lighter or matches without authorization
  • Lending of ID's and gate pass to others
  • Cutting classes
  • Intentional/Unintentional inflicting of harm to himself/herself
  • Stealing or attempted theft
  • Bringing pornographic materials
  • Bringing of gambling materials
  • Assault or threat to a person in authority while performing duty

Penalties for Level 3 Offenses

  • 1st Offense of an infraction - Parent Notification + 3 demerits
  • 2nd Offense of the same infraction - Parent Notification + Deprivation from attending the subject where offense was committed + 6 demerits
  • 3rd Offense of the same infraction onwards - Parent Notification + Prohibition from attending 1 day of synchronous classes + 9 demerits

LEVEL 4 - Acts of Unbecoming of an HIMC Student

  • Any form of cheating (non-transmutable zero + penalty)
  • Any kind of bullying (Implementing Rules and Regulations of Republic Act #10627 or the Anti-Bullying Act of 2013)
  • Violation of the Data Privacy Act
  • Any violation of the HIMC Child Protection Policy (Implementing Rules and Regulations in compliance to Section 7 of DepEd Order 40, 2012)
  • Threatening/Harassment of any individual in relation to HIMC both online and offline
  • Conduct unbecoming as manifested by (but not limited to): any form of pornography, smoking cigarettes / vaping, extortion, exhibiting signs of under the influence of drugs or alcohol, initiating fights or gambling, backbiting or rumor mongering
  • Possession of deadly weapons

Penalties for Level 4 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 1 day suspension + 26 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + 2 days suspension + Conduct probation + 26 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + Removal from attending synchronous classes for the remainder of the quarter + warning for dropping from school roll + 26 demerits
