LEVEL 1 - Negligence to Fulfill Basic Duties

  • Tardiness
  • Unexpected absences
  • Wearing inappropriate attire
  • Failure to submit correspondence / have materials a 3rd time
  • Sleeping in class
  • Violation of SOP's
  • Misbehavior
  • Improper haircut
  • No ID or No gate pass
  • Loitering during class hours
  • Littering
  • Trespassing
  • Disruption of classes.

Penalties for Level 1 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Reminder + 1 demerit
  • 2nd Offense - Reminder + 2 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Reprimand + 3 demerits
  • 4th Offense onwards - Parent Notification + 4 demerits

LEVEL 2 - Minor Behavioral Infractions

  • Any disruptive behavior/audio/video/material
  • Using vulgar language or indecent publication
  • Bringing of electronic gadgets
  • Staying in the school premises after school hours
  • Putting temporary marks on their bodies
  • Putting on make-up or colored nail polish
  • Fancy haircut or loud hair color
  • Holding unauthorized meetings or rallies
  • Vandalism

Penalties for Level 2 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Reminder + 2 demerit
  • 2nd Offense - Reminder + 4 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Reprimand + 6 demerits
  • 4th Offense onwards - Parent Notification + Deprivation + 8 demerits

LEVEL 3 - Acts of Disrespect and Dishonesty

  • Any form of disrespect or malicious act
  • Tampering of any school document/property
  • Any form of dishonesty (forgery, identity theft)
  • Bringing of cigarettes, lighter or matches
  • Lending of ID's and gate pass
  • Cutting classes
  • Inflicting of harm to himself/herself
  • Stealing or attempted theft
  • Bringing pornographic materials
  • Bringing of gambling materials
  • Assault or threat to a person in authority

Penalties for Level 3 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 3 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + Deprivation + 6 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + Prohibition + 9 demerits

LEVEL 4 - Acts of Unbecoming of an HIMC Student

  • Any form of cheating
  • Any kind of bullying
  • Violation of the Data Privacy Act
  • Violation of the HIMC Child Protection Policy
  • Threatening/Harassment of any individual
  • Smoking cigarettes / vaping, extortion, under influence of drugs or alcohol, initiating fights or gambling, backbiting or rumor mongering
  • Possession of deadly weapons

Penalties for Level 4 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 1 day suspension + 26 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + 2 days suspension + Conduct probation + 26 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + Removal from classes + warning + 26 demerits
